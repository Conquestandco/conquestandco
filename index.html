The Automotive Aspiration

Bridging the Gap


Based in New South Wales, Conquest & Co. Trading Pty Ltd is a premier B2B wholesale distributor specializing in high-end automotive parts and accessories for the luxury vehicle market.

We provide comprehensive, precision-engineered upgrade solutions designed to elevate prestige vehicles beyond their original factory specifications. By sourcing and distributing only the highest quality aftermarket and OEM-equivalent components, we empower our B2B partners to maximize vehicle equity.

Core Trade Offerings

Specification Conversions

Upgrade base or mid-tier luxury vehicles to flagship, high-specification trim levels. Supplying workshops with the exact components needed to match premier models.

Exterior Facelifts

Modernize older-model vehicles to seamlessly mirror the exterior design language of the newest releases. From aerodynamic body kits and laser lighting systems to custom grilles.

Interior Modernisation

Next-generation digital instrument clusters, enhanced infotainment retrofits, premium upholstery, ambient lighting kits, and modernized steering wheel assemblies.
